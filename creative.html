The Window 2026
About

A micro short film exploring the uncertainty of timing, dreams, and the places that stay on our minds. Through fragmented flashes of memory, the piece captures the feeling of wondering whether an opportunity is arriving, fading, or simply waiting for the right moment.

Scuffers, With Love 2024
About

A spec ad created to showcase Scuffers Co.'s "With Love" product line — a high-end collection built around intention and craftsmanship. The project served as a creative pivot toward branded content, exploring a more refined visual style and narrative approach rooted in product-driven storytelling. The accompanying photo set was built to reflect Scuffers Co.'s minimal, creative brand identity — focused on simplicity, soft light, and clean composition to let the product speak without distraction. Each image intentional, understated, and on-brand: a blend of their identity with a subtle creative edge.

My Postcard from NY 2025
About

A personal document of time spent in New York City, shot with the same intentionality as any branded project but without the structure. Less of a travel film, more of a memory. A record of what it feels like to move through a city that refuses to stand still.
