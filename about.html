Peter Millar

Digital Media Producer

2025–Present

I produce and shoot all video and photo content for Peter Millar's digital channels — developing concepts, directing on set, and delivering the final edit entirely in-house. The content is built for organic social but regularly gets pulled into paid digital, email, and campaign work by the broader marketing team.

Every shoot runs lean — no outside production crew, no agency middleman. I coordinate directly with the VP of Creative and the creative department to align each piece with the seasonal campaign, the brand identity, and the platform it's going to live on. The result is content that looks and performs at a paid level, made with the resources of an in-house team.

I shoot both motion and stills across product, lifestyle, and editorial contexts — adapting approach and format to whatever the brief calls for.

603 Studio LLC

Founder

2025–Present

603 Studio is my production company — founded in mid-2025 after closing a prior partnership, operational from day one.

I produce video and photo content for lifestyle brands and local businesses, running every aspect of production independently: client communication, creative concepting, location scouting, shooting, and post-production delivery. When a project requires more, I bring in a trusted network of freelance photographers, videographers, and creatives — directing those collaborations the same way I direct my own work.

Current clients include the launch of North Carolina's first Triumph Motorcycle dealership and the state's largest construction company, producing brand assets across their parent company's website. Project work extends into the New York market.

Half Melted Productions LLC

Co-Owner

2023–2025

I co-founded Half Melted in early 2023 and ran it for two years as a full-service photo and video production company — taking on lifestyle, entertainment, and brand clients across North Carolina.

During that time the company partnered with two talent agencies, directed media for Raleigh's Glenwood South Entertainment District — growing their social presence to 10K followers in six months and reaching 243K accounts in three months — and produced the promotional visuals that helped bring Rockingham Speedway back to a NASCAR contract for 2025.

The largest production of that run was Loud Luxury's nationwide Fall 2024 tour. I managed multi-camera event coverage across multiple tour stops — handling concept, crew coordination, on-location shooting, and post-production from start to finish.

Birds Eye Productions

Founder

2021–2023

Birds Eye was my first production company, built while I was still at North Carolina State University.

I ran it as a solo operation — shooting and editing photo and video content for 50+ local businesses across industries: apparel, hospitality, food and beverage, entertainment. Every project was handled start to finish, which meant learning every part of the production process quickly and without a safety net.

The network built through Birds Eye led directly to the clients and collaborators that made Half Melted possible.
