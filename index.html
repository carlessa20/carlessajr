Bio

Drawn to brands that treat craft as a non-negotiable. Where visual language is intentional and nothing feels forced.

Carl Essa Jr is a videographer and photographer producing high-production content for lifestyle and apparel brands. Currently the Digital Media Producer at Peter Millar - shooting and editing video for their organic social, paid digital, and campaign work.

Before that: co-founded a production company, built a studio from scratch, shot across North Carolina, New York, and more - over 25 brands, a nationwide tour, and a short film that sold out two theaters.

FAA-licensed drone pilot. Premiere Pro · Final Cut Pro · Lightroom. Motion and stills.

Based in Raleigh, NC.
