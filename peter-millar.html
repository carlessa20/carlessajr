Intentionally Done Right 2025
About

To support the launch of Peter Millar's "Intentionally Done Right" campaign, I helped tell the story behind the collection through an interview with the Chief Creative Officer, exploring the inspiration and craftsmanship behind the Spring Collection. I then carried that message into our partnership with Sam Burns, using the relationship between practice and tournament play to show that doing something right is built through preparation, reflection, and intention - not just the final result.

About

The Spring/Summer collection was made to be introduced through a campaign but lived in through social and digital. Set against a Weekend in the Hamptons, the story paired relaxed coastal moments with intentional product details, turning the collection into an experience rather than an advertisement.
