Rockingham Speedway
Role Videographer · Photographer · Editor
Category Brand Campaign · NASCAR
Client Rockingham Speedway
Year 2024 – 2025
Overview

Rockingham Speedway hadn't seen NASCAR in over a decade. Brought in alongside business partner Ian Gillen to build the visual case for its revival — documenting the rebuild through track events, open houses, and investor showcases. The work wasn't just promotional, it was instrumental. By Spring 2025, the owner had secured a NASCAR contract and The Rock was back.

Videographer: Carl Essa Jr & Ian Gillen Photographer: Carl Essa Jr Editor: Carl Essa Jr & Ian Gillen

About

After years of collecting dust, we assisted in the revival of Rockingham Speedway. Through developing target marketing and high-impact media that worked its image for NASCAR's return — this is the film that documented the payoff. The Xfinity Series back at The Rock, exactly where it belongs.
